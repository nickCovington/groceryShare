Why 'Grocery Share'?

I made this tool because I love sharing exotic new foods & recipes with my buddies.

I figured other people might enjoy the experience as well.

Class Take-Aways:

Before taking web programming, I had no clue how to connect to a database & fetch/store data back and forth between the client & the server.

I look forward to developing more sites in the future which implement more & more complex database schemas.

Through this project, I also learned the joys of using external libraries to quickly speed up various processes.

Bootstrap, in particular, has shown me the wonders of significantly sprucing up a website by just adding a few classnames to an HTML tag.
